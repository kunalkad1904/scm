SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT FOR AGRICULTURE

• ABOUT THE PROJECT •

The supply chain management project for agriculture aims to improve the efficiency and profitability of the agriculture supply chain. This project will involve implementing a digital platform to connect farmers, suppliers, and buyers, allowing them to easily share information and coordinate their activities. We have also aim to develop a supply chain management system for the agriculture sector. The main goal of this system is to streamline the supply chain and improve the efficiency of the overall process.

© FEATURES

  • Inventory management
  • Order tracking
  • Invoices tracking
  • Quality control
  • Quality control and certification
  • Automated ordering and invoicing
  • Logistics/Products management

© BENEFITS

By implementing this system, farmers and other stakeholders in the agriculture supply chain can expect:

  • Improved visibility and transparency in the supply chain
  • Reduced costs through more efficient processes
  • Better quality control and food safety
  • Increase transparency and trust between stakeholders
  • Facilitate access to new markets, customers and products
  • Increased profitability through better management of inventory and orders
